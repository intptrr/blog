This is the first post on this blog. I'm starting it as a place to put down notes
I'd otherwise lose: things I read, things I built, things I changed my mind about.

The goal isn't to publish on a schedule or build an audience. It's to think more
clearly by writing things down, and to have a stable URL to point at when someone
asks "what do you think about X?"

A few rules I'm setting for myself:

- Write the post for past-me. The version of me who didn't know the thing.
- Ship it ugly. Polish later, or don't.
- No analytics, no comments, no popups. If you want to reach me, my email is
  in the footer.

That's it. Onwards.
